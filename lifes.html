Hand Washing

Wet your hands with clean, running water (warm or cold), turn off the tap, and apply soap. Lather your hands by rubbing them together with the soap. Lather the backs of your hands, between your fingers, and under your nails. Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds.

Better Air

When the pandemic hit, city streets were eerily quiet. Passenger traffic plummeted, and traffic-related emissions—particularly carbon dioxide and nitrogen dioxide—dropped accordingly. Gas-powered vehicles directly emit CO2 and NO2 as combustion by-products. CO2 is a greenhouse gas that plays a major role in global warming. NO2 plays a key role in atmospheric reactions that produce ozone and fine particulates, both of which harm health

Healty Diet Plan

People who eat a well-balanced diet tend to be healthier with stronger immune systems and lower risk of chronic illnesses and infectious diseases. So you should eat a variety of fresh and unprocessed foods every day to get the vitamins, minerals, dietary fibre, protein and antioxidants your body needs
